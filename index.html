About

Javier aka Hahvee is one of Sydney's electronic music producers with over ten years experience in creating wall licking drops.

He’s worked on a variety of projects like the Harry Potter Parody show “The Philosopher’s Strip”; music for Miss Burlesque Australia; and has just finished working with Etcetera Etcetera, one of the contestants from Ru Paul’s Drag Race Down Under.

HAHVEE is currently working on his debut EP, and will be releasing it into the world later 2021.

Stay tuned!
